Welcome to my website!

My name is Art Flachsenhar, or Artie Flash for short. I am a Software Engineer and a Magna Cum Laude graduate from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.

I am primarily a back-end developer, but I've been independently learning front-end development in my free time. This website it my first front-end endeavor, and welcome any feedback (positive or negative).

As I advance in my career, I will update this site to reflect what I've done and what I've learned. The most profound realization I have had since learning Computer Science, is just how much technology I have taken for granted. As Arthur C. Clarke put it,

"Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic."

The prospect of learning more about the technology we already have, in addition to all of the 'magic' that will be developed in my lifetime, sparks inspiration and excitement for this industry.
